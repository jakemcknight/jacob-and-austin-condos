## Stay in the Loop

- **Convention Center Redesign** -- New renderings revealed with plans closing April 1. Reopening targeted for SXSW 2029. The redesigned space doubles capacity with underground construction and ground-floor community access year-round. [Read more](https://communityimpact.com/austin/south-central-austin/government/2025/02/25/austin-convention-center-redevelopment-design-revealed/)

- **SXSW Begins** -- Festival kicks off next two weekends downtown. [View schedule](https://www.sxsw.com/schedule-overview/)

- **Seaholm Market Plaza** -- Transformation project underway for Third Street and Power Plant Drive, breaking ground summer 2025 with approximately three-month duration. [Public input](https://speakupaustin.org/e65606)
