## Data Dive

Downtown Austin's residential landscape continues expanding with approximately 9,800 rental apartments currently available. During 2025-2026, roughly 2,400 new rental units are entering the market as construction projects complete leasing phases.

In the condo sector, only one new tower delivered during this timeframe, contributing 367 units -- predominantly sold with move-ins underway -- bringing total downtown condos to around 5,400 units.

## Stay in the Loop

**The Code on S Lamar** -- A rebranded condo project emphasizing flexible ownership options: full-time residency, part-time occupancy, or full-time short-term rental operations. Sales begin next week. [Learn more](https://www.thecodeaustin.com/)

**Townlake YMCA Redevelopment** -- The planned complex includes five structures: a newly constructed YMCA facility, standalone affordable housing building, and three 400-foot market-rate condo towers. [Read more](https://communityimpact.com/austin/south-central-austin/development/2025/10/17/ymca-details-plans-for-redeveloped-townlake-facility-with-condo-towers-affordable-housing/)

**IHOP Foreclosure on Cesar Chavez** -- The property near Rainey Street owned by Nate Paul faces a foreclosure auction on December 2. The 0.8-acre site is appraised at $26.7 million. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/10/20/nate-paul-empire-dwindles-austin-foreclosure.html)
