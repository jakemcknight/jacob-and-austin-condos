## Data Dive

Downtown demand increased in the second half of 2025. Median prices rose while cost per square foot declined, signaling buyer interest in larger units. Days-on-market increased, reflecting active but selective purchasing behavior. Two and three-bedroom units are rebounding strongly while one-bedrooms continue underperforming.

**For buyers:** One-bedroom condos remain under pressure and negotiation opportunities exist for aggressive offers. Two and three-bedroom units command firmer pricing with year-over-year gains.

**For sellers:** One-bedrooms require sharp pricing due to supply and rental competition. Two and three-bedroom units benefit from strong demand and are regaining value.

## Stay in the Loop

**Red River Free Week (Jan 9-10)** -- Free admission at downtown venues for live music during this annual celebration of Austin's music scene.

**Shiner's Saloon** -- The demolition of the historic saloon is awaiting a Historic Landmark Commission decision. The site is positioned on Congress Avenue for potential redevelopment.

**6th Street Redesign** -- Alternative 3 has been selected as the recommended redesign, featuring the widest pedestrian zones, two lanes, parking, and no bike lanes. [Share your input](https://speakupaustin.org/sixthstreet)
