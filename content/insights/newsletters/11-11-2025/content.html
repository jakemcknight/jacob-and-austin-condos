STAY IN THE LOOP

 

  • Antone’s secures its future – the legendary blues club extended its lease through 2075 and will add a second-floor blues museum honoring icons like B.B. King, Muddy Waters, Ray Charles, James Brown, and Stevie Ray Vaughan.

  • The Twelve Thirty Club set to remake a downtown landmark – Sam Fox and Justin Timberlake will turn the old post office at 210 W. Sixth into a four-story restaurant, speakeasy, and private club. The project breaks ground in 2026 with plans to open in 2027.

  • Daydreamer Coffee opens at Paseo - A new cafe is officially open on the ground floor of Paseo apartments on Rainey Street. It’s nice to see some variety on this strip!
