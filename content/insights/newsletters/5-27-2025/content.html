STAY IN THE LOOP

 


  • City approves funding for 3 caps and 2 stitches over I35 - support columns will now be added to the I35 project for caps between Cesar Chavez and Fourth St, Fourth and Seventh, and 11th to 12th. This will allow an additional 15+ acres in downtown Austin!

  • W 6th St tower continuing to move forward - Kairoi’s proposed 66-story residential high rise at the corner of Rio Grande and 6th was approved for the density bonus program. No word on construction or timeline of the project. Let me know if you’d like to stay in the loop!

  • Swedish Hill to open location downtown - coming fall 2025, Swedish Hill will continue its expansion to the ground floor of 415 Colorado (new apartment tower).
