DATA DIVE

 

Downtown listings are telling a clear story:

  • Active: 104 days on market at $809/SF

  • Failed: 125 days on market at $837/SF

  • Closed: 83 days on market at $670/SF (41 days when sold at/above list)


Notably, 60 of 68 sales over the past three months closed below list at 91% of original asking.
It’s a reminder that well-positioned units create urgency, while overpricing leads to slower absorption.

  • Why Donn’s Depot is still my favorite Austin bar - Read up on this charming old train depot turned piano bar. A timeless spot that still stands strong while the rest of Austin changes.
