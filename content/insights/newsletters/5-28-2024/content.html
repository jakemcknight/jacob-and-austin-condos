Data Dive from the Downtown Austin Alliance

Downtown continues to grow as a residential neighborhood. 1,303 new residential units were delivered in 2023 and 3,062 more currently under construction. This includes rental apartments and condos. Downtown’s population grew by approximately 850 people over the last year. Residents aged 25 to 34 continue to represent the largest share of population in downtown, however, from 2022 to 2023 the median age of downtown increased from 35 to 37.  More insights found in the recently released “State of Downtown Austin 2024” presented by Downtown Austin Alliance.
