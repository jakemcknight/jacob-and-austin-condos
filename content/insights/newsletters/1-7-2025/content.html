STAY IN THE LOOP

 

  • Austin ranks 5th in growth metro by U-Haul who ranks “growth metros” by net gain, which contrasts the number of one-way equipment entering a city versus those leaving. Texas was the 2nd growth state behind South Carolina

  • Waterloo Records is moving to a larger location - the new space will have more parking, space to host concerts, and should be ready for SXSW shows this year! Endeavor purchased the current site in 2019, still no known plans but will go vertical.

  • The first move-ins for three major residential towers - The Modern, ATX Tower, & 415 Colorado are scheduled for March.
