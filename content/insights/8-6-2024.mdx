# 70 Rainey Case Study, 360 Condo Balcony Reconstruction

## Data Dive

I conducted an analysis for a 70 Rainey Condos owner examining the impact of ongoing construction on the Rainey Street District. The study concluded that ongoing construction has reduced home values in the district by 4.67%. Reach out if you'd like details on the methodology.

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 206 (down 4% from 215)
- **New Listings:** 16 (down 16% from 19)
- **Pending:** 7 (up 57% from 3)
- **Closed:** 4 (no change from 4)

New supply decreased while demand increased.

## Stay in the Loop

**The Modern Tops Out** -- The final condo development on Rainey Street has reached its structural completion. The 320-unit, 56-story building sold out immediately and is expected to complete in 2025.

**First & Last New Development of 2024?** -- Hotel Trinity, a 13-story Marriott at East 5th & Trinity, is set for site demolition by year-end, with an anticipated 2026 opening.
