# HOA Yearly Increases, The Kitchen by Musk

## Data Dive

Downtown Austin HOA fees have increased at an average annual rate of 4.12% over the past five years (4.68% annually over a decade). The Austonian building specifically shows a projected yearly increase of approximately 2.88%.

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 215 (up 2% from 210)
- **New Listings:** 19 (up 16% from 16)
- **Pending:** 3 (down 57% from 7)
- **Closed:** 4 (down 43% from 7)

New supply increased while demand softened.
