# Q1 Update, Leland South Congress, Reggae Fest

## Data Dive

A quarterly comparison showing market performance between Q1 2024 and the prior year period. The downtown real estate market is experiencing a slowdown. Days on market for units have increased, providing buyers with more negotiating power and greater selection at lower prices compared to the previous year.

## Stay in the Loop

**Leland South Congress Condos** -- A new pre-construction investment opportunity is in the Letter of Intent phase. Prospective buyers can place a fully refundable $1,000 deposit for first purchase access.

**415 Colorado Tops Out** -- The building is 45 stories and will contain 328 residential units alongside 110,000 square feet of office space with ground-level retail. [Read more at Austin Towers](https://austin.towers.net/415-colorado-apartment-and-office-tower-tops-out-in-downtown-austin/)

**Austin Reggae Festival** -- April 19-21 at Auditorium Shores. Weekend passes are $75; day passes start at $20. [Visit the festival website](https://www.austinreggaefest.com/)
