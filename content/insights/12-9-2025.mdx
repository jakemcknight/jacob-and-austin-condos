## Stay in the Loop

**$20B in Infrastructure Projects** -- Major infrastructure projects are underway including I-35 expansion, airport expansion, convention center redevelopment, and the public light rail system. Expected completion by 2033. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/12/07/2025-update-austin-infrastructure-projects.html)

**Shiner's Saloon Redevelopment** -- The historic saloon at W 5th & Congress is set for redevelopment with a recently approved site plan. The Austonian's northern condos are most affected by the change. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/12/08/downtown-austin-shiners-saloon-redevelopment.html)

**Autonomous Vehicle Safety** -- Waymo autonomous vehicles are involved in 91% fewer serious crashes compared to human drivers, per a NY Times analysis. [Read more](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/12/02/opinion/self-driving-cars.html)
