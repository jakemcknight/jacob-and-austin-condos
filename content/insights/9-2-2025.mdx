## Stay in the Loop

- **One Lady Bird:** Site prep has commenced for a 17-story apartment and condo building just south of the river on S 1st St, featuring restaurant and retail space on two floors. [Read more](https://austin.urbanize.city/post/one-ladybird-lake-related-residential)

- **Waterline tops out:** Waterline in the Rainey Street District has reached its final height of 1,025 feet (74 stories), with an anticipated Fall 2026 opening. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/08/01/waterline-tops-out-reaches-its-full-height-austin.html)

- **Convention Center ballot measure:** Austin United PAC is pursuing a ballot measure to redirect Convention Center funds toward music, arts, cultural, and outdoor tourism initiatives. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/07/29/austin-united-pac-halt-convention-center-project.html)
