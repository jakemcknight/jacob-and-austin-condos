# Downtown Quadruples, Deep Eddy Cabaret Now Historic

## Data Dive

Downtown living has increased 4x in value since 2000, while the broader Austin-Round Rock metropolitan statistical area has doubled over the same period.

## Stay in the Loop

**Project Connect Construction Kicks Off in 2027** -- Phase 1 of the light rail project will connect Bergstrom airport through Riverside Drive with branches extending to Downtown, UT campus, and South Congress. Train operations are expected by 2033. [Read more at Austin Business Journal](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/03/25/project-connect-light-rail-austin.html)

**Deep Eddy Cabaret Secures Historic Designation** -- The 70-year-old dive bar receives historic status, enabling preservation through tax incentives, grants, and future development protections. [Read more at Austin Business Journal](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/03/25/austin-deep-eddy-cabaret-historic-designation.html)

**Mexic-Arte Museum Plans a Long-Awaited Renovation** -- Downtown's official Mexican, Latino, and Latin American art institution announces renovation plans. [Read more at Austin Towers](https://austin.towers.net/downtown-austins-mexic-arte-museum-plans-a-long-awaited-renovation/)
