## Stay in the Loop

**Convention Center Redevelopment** -- Austin United PAC initiated ballot signature collection to pause or cancel the project, though city officials maintain the initiative remains on schedule and budget. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/10/13/convention-center-redevelopment-united-austin-pac.html)

**Base Power Factory at Former Statesman Site** -- Base Power launched its initial Texas manufacturing facility at the former Statesman location following $1 billion in funding, while broader South Central Waterfront District plans remain in limbo. [Read more](https://communityimpact.com/austin/south-central-austin/development/2025/10/09/base-power-launches-first-texas-factory-with-1b-funding/)

**Congress Avenue Redesign** -- The initial construction phase begins in 2026 focusing on Cesar Chavez to 7th Street, incorporating wider sidewalks, protected cycling infrastructure, and enhanced landscaping. [Read more](https://downtownaustin.com/what-we-do/our-services/congress-avenue/)
