## Data Dive

With construction in the Rainey Street District wrapping up by 2026, the area will total roughly 4,700 residential units within just a few blocks. This makes it the most concentrated pocket of housing in Texas and raises questions about potential ranking among the densest neighborhoods in the U.S.
