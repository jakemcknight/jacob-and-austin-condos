# Cash Buyers, Ziki & Foxtrot Close

## Data Dive

Approximately 52% of downtown condo purchases in 2023 involved cash payments. Out of 175 transactions, 90 were paid in cash and 85 were financed.

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 215 (up 1%)
- **New Listings:** 18 (down 18%)
- **Pending:** 9 (down 50%)
- **Closed:** 8 (down 20%)

New supply and absorption continued to cool, evening each other out.

## Stay in the Loop

**Business Closures** -- Ziki Kitchen food trucks shut down without explanation. Foxtrot suddenly closed as well; the company announced reopening select locations but has provided no update regarding the downtown Austin location.

**Affordable Senior Housing Downtown** -- A five-story, 83-unit affordable senior housing complex is planned for St. Martin's Lutheran Church's parking lot at 1500 Rio Grande. [Read more at Austin Towers](https://austin.towers.net/downtown-austin-affordable-senior-housing-planned-st-martins/)

**Architecture Feature** -- An interview with Michael Hsu Office of Architecture, the firm responsible for designing 44 East, Headliners Club, The Kitchen (6th & Guadalupe), and other notable local projects. [Read the interview at Austin Business Journal](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/06/06/q-a-michael-hsu-office-austin-architecture.html)
