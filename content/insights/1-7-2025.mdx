## Data Dive

Downtown Austin condo market showed its first price decline since 2016. The median value dropped from $827/SF in 2023 to $771/SF in 2024.

*Decade-long pricing chart visualizing the trend*

The year-end supply decrease was seasonal, with expectations for increased inventory through spring.

## Stay in the Loop

- **Austin Ranked 5th Growth Metro** -- Austin ranked 5th among growth metros by U-Haul. Texas was second nationally for growth state status. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/01/03/austin-texas-u-haul-data.html)

- **Waterloo Records Relocating** -- Moving to a larger venue with expanded parking and concert hosting capacity, targeting SXSW readiness. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/01/02/waterloo-records-austin-icon-ownership-sold.html)

- **Three Towers Moving In** -- The Modern, ATX Tower, and 415 Colorado are all scheduled for March move-ins.
