## Stay in the Loop

**I-35 Capping Program Delayed** -- Austin delayed this week's vote on the I-35 capping program. The decision has been postponed to March 2025 regarding funding for 25 additional acres of downtown community amenities.

**The Republic Hits 50% Leased** -- The 48-story downtown office building [The Republic](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/10/17/downtown-austin-hotel-trinity-renovation.html) is now 50% leased and expects to top out in January, with opening planned for next summer.

**Austin's Clean Drinking Water** -- Testing shows Austin drinking water contains little to no "forever chemicals," with levels cleaner than EPA standards.
