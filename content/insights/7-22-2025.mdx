## Data Dive

In 2025, downtown Austin condos are selling at approximately 9% below listing price on average, continuing the trend from 2023-2024.

**Quick Links:** [New Listings](https://portal.onehome.com/en-US/share/1115795b26320) | [All Active Listings](https://portal.onehome.com/en-US/properties/map)

## Stay in the Loop

- **Hobby Building redevelopment:** A ground lease has been secured for the city block, which is expected to be redeveloped with a priority on workforce housing for teachers, nurses, and police. The project has a two-year timeline.

- **Hideout Theatre relocating:** The Hideout Theatre is leaving its Congress Avenue location (which will become a bar) and moving to Art Hub ATX near St. Edwards.

- **South Central Waterfront District:** Six acres at South Congress and Riverside have been approved by the Planning Commission, with a timeline extending beyond 2030.
