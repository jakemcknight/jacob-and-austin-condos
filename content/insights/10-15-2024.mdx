## Data Dive

Recent media reports claim Austin sold approximately half the homes compared to 2019, representing the lowest levels in over 20 years. I examined downtown market data going back to 2000 to put this in perspective.

**Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot** (Last Update: 10/1/2024)

| Metric | Current | Previous | Change |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| Total Active Listings | 191 | 194 | -2% |
| New Listings | 14 | 20 | -30% |
| Pending | 14 | 11 | +21% |
| Closed | 3 | 7 | -57% |

Decrease in supply and increase in demand.

## Stay in the Loop

**Sixth & Blanco Secures $193M** -- A mixed-use development on West Sixth Street has secured [$193 million in construction financing](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/10/08/west-sixth-blanco-austin.html) for $10M condos, a hotel, and retail space.

**Congress Avenue Urban Design Initiative** -- The [Congress Avenue Urban Design Initiative](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/6fe940043db14dd2b9fc1e44f82dafe0) is seeking public feedback on pedestrian improvements along Congress Avenue.

**Downtown Holiday Stroll** -- The 30th Annual Downtown Holiday Stroll attracts over 10,000 Austinites each year. Sponsorship opportunities are available.
