# Independent Strong Growth, Ritz-Carlton a No Go

## Data Dive

The Independent condominium building has experienced the strongest appreciation among downtown Austin's residential towers over the past five years, with a 50.26% total adjustment (8.48% annually). The broader downtown market saw 43.23% growth over the same period (8.06% annually).

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 203 (up 6% from 192)
- **New Listings:** 25 (up 19% from 21)
- **Pending:** 25 (down 4% from 26)
- **Closed:** 7 (down 30% from 10)

More inventory entered the market while absorption decreased.

## Stay in the Loop

**Future of Downtown Event** -- The Downtown Alliance is presenting their 2024 State of Downtown report this Wednesday evening, covering development, employment, culture, and mobility findings. I plan to attend.

**After-Hour Activities Driving Recovery** -- According to cell phone activity data, economic diversity -- including entertainment, restaurants, retail, and tourism -- is playing a significant role in the post-pandemic urban recovery of downtown Austin.

**Ritz-Carlton Project Delayed** -- The proposed 64-story hotel tower on Rainey Street faces significant obstacles. The developer is contending with legal challenges and complications involving Nashville-based projects, making near-term development unlikely.
