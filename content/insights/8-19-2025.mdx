## Stay in the Loop

- **Kappo Kappo at Austin Proper:** Austin Proper's newest venue offers 11-course counter style dining.

- **6th Street barrier redesign:** The concrete barriers on 6th Street are receiving an artistic redesign by local artists. ([Downtown Austin Alliance](https://www.linkedin.com/company/downtown-austin-alliance/))

- **Valet parking on 6th:** Valet parking service begins August 22 on 6th Street from I-35 to Congress, funded by the Pecan Street Owners Association.
