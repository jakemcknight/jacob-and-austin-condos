## Data Dive

Q1 2025 showed stronger activity compared to the prior year. Transaction volume increased from 34 to 43 closings. Days on market rose from 94 to 148 days. The closed median price per square foot grew 3.5%, moving from $774/SF to $801/SF.

## Stay in the Loop

- **Wilson Tower Delayed** -- The condo tower on E 5th St awaits market improvement before proceeding.

- **Royal Blue Closing** -- The Congress Avenue grocery location will not renew its lease due to developer interest. The concept had operated at 60-70% of pre-pandemic revenue.

- **Belvedere Groundbreaking** -- A three-building condo development in Clarksville near Better Half Coffee, with estimated completion in August 2027.
