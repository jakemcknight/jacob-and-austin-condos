## Data Dive

A comparison of the first half of 2024 versus the first half of 2023 shows the median price per square foot increased, with the median square footage sold rising from 1,050 sf to 1,200 sf.

**Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot** (Last Update: 8/20/2024)

| Metric | Current | Previous | Change |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| Total Active Listings | 189 | 201 | -6% |
| New Listings | 10 | 14 | -29% |
| Pending | 14 | 15 | -7% |
| Closed | 4 | 5 | -20% |

Overall slowdown with supply and demand both decreasing.

## Stay in the Loop

**Paseo Tops Out at 48 Stories** -- [Paseo apartment tower](https://austin.towers.net/paseo-apartment-tower-celebrates-topping-out-on-rainey-street/?pro=caitlyn@towers.net) celebrates topping out on Rainey Street. The 557-unit building is expected to be completed in late 2025.

**Katz's Deli Returns to Downtown** -- [Katz's Deli](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/08/27/katzs-deli-austin-nate-paul-site.html), the historic deli that closed in 2011 after 32 years, is reopening at the former Rio nightclub location on West 6th.
