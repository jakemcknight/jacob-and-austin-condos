# 360 Condo Discounts, Pecan Festival, Duncan Park Opens

## Data Dive

360 Condos are experiencing price reductions because of exterior patio deterioration. Construction work is anticipated to extend through the following summer, prompting some property owners to sell. Those who can tolerate the ongoing work may benefit from future appreciation.

- **Pending:** 26 (up 100%)

## Stay in the Loop

**Pecan Street Festival** -- A free two-day arts festival held twice yearly on 6th Street. The spring edition occurs May 4-5. [Visit the festival website](https://pecanstreetfestival.org/)

**Vesper Condos Completed** -- Units remain available for purchase and investor permits are still offered. The building permits 30+ day rentals, which is uncommon for new downtown high-rises. [Learn more about Vesper](https://vesperatx.com/)

**Duncan Park Opening** -- Recently opened along Shoal Creek between 9th and 10th Streets, adjacent to a dirt BMX park. [View park details](https://www.austintexas.gov/department/duncan-park-improvements)
