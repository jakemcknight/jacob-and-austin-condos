The first half of 2025 outperformed H1 2024 in transaction volume. Smaller units moving at cheaper prices are the story of 2025 so far.

## Stay in the Loop

- **Waterloo Records site development:** Endeavor Real Estate has proposed a nine-story mixed-use building with 93 residential units at the NW corner of W 6th and N Lamar.

- **Michi Japanese Deli/Sushi:** A new restaurant is taking over the former Royal Blue location near 6th and Congress.

- **ATX Tower glass panel incident:** Another glass panel fell from the mixed-use development; no injuries were reported. ([KUT Austin](https://www.kut.org/))
