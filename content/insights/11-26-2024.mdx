## Data Dive

Examining downtown condo pricing trends reveals an interesting story. Median condo values increased from $720,000 in 2023 to $763,500 in 2024 -- a 6% gain. However, price per square foot actually declined from $827/sf to $754/sf, a 9% decrease.

This discrepancy reflects changing inventory: the median square footage sold rose from 1,060 sf in 2023 to 1,200 sf in 2024. Larger units are trading at lower per-square-foot prices, while overall median values climb. Supply has increased while demand slows.
