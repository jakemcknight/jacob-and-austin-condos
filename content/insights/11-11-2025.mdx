## Data Dive

The Rainey Street District will feature approximately 4,700 residential units within a few blocks once construction concludes in 2026. This concentration raises questions about whether the area could become one of the densest neighborhoods in the United States. Demand continues matching new housing supply.

## Stay in the Loop

**Antone's Secures Its Future** -- The legendary blues club extended its lease through 2075 and will add a second-floor blues museum honoring icons like B.B. King, Muddy Waters, Ray Charles, James Brown, and Stevie Ray Vaughan. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/11/10/antones-50-year-lease-museum-blues-downtown-austin.html)

**Twelve Thirty Club Coming to Austin** -- Sam Fox and Justin Timberlake will transform the old post office at 210 W. Sixth into a four-story restaurant, speakeasy, and private club launching in 2027. [Read more](https://www.forbes.com/sites/katiebell/2025/11/10/sam-fox-and-justin-timberlake-bring-the-twelve-thirty-club-to-austin/)

**Daydreamer Coffee Opens at Paseo** -- A new cafe opened on the ground floor of Paseo apartments on Rainey Street. [Check it out](https://www.instagram.com/p/DQxFrWgAaSU/)
