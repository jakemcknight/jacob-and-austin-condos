## Data Dive

Should you buy a 2-bedroom instead of a 1-bedroom when your budget allows? The data suggests yes -- 2 bedrooms are generally more desirable and easier to resell. Market data from 2024 shows 1-bedrooms remain listed for a median of 91 days versus just 61 days for 2-bedrooms in downtown Austin.

**Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot** (Last Update: 10/29/2024)

| Metric | Current | Previous | Change |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| Total Active Listings | 189 | 198 | -5% |
| New Listings | 14 | 18 | -22% |
| Pending | 15 | 11 | +27% |
| Closed | 4 | 4 | 0% |

Decrease in supply and increase in demand.

## Stay in the Loop

**The Kitchen Opens at 6G** -- The Kitchen by Kimbal Musk opens on the ground floor of 6G with a public opening on November 14th.

**Remedy Bar at 360 Condos** -- The old Trifecta has reopened as Remedy, a neighborhood corner bar at 360 Condos featuring trivia, open mic nights, and wine tastings.

**Willie Nelson Mural Building** -- The historic Willie Nelson mural building near the Red River district, damaged by fire in February, is scheduled for demolition. Future plans for the site remain unknown.
