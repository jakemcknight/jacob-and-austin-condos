## Stay in the Loop

**Congress Avenue Urban Design Initiative** -- Construction has begun on Congress Ave. Phase 1 is underway with each block requiring approximately 3 months, with final completion targeted for 2027. [View the Story Map](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/6fe940043db14dd2b9fc1e44f82dafe0)

**Five Iron Golf at 5th & Congress** -- Golf simulators, training facilities, a bar, restaurant, and entertainment venue are signing a lease at 5th & Congress. [Read more](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/five-iron-golf-signs-lease-130200943.html)

**Two New Towers Near MLK & Rio Grande** -- A proposed 36-story condo-hotel and 34-story apartment with ground-floor retail and a pedestrian paseo are planned near MLK & Rio Grande. [View the planning document](https://services.austintexas.gov/edims/document.cfm?id=466592)
