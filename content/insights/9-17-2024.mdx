## Stay in the Loop

**Earthquake Felt Downtown** -- A [5.1 magnitude earthquake](https://www.reddit.com/r/Austin/comments/1filq75/usgs_report_of_48_magnitude_west_texas_earthquake/) struck near west Texas oil and gas operations. I felt it in my unit downtown.

**Congress Avenue Properties Hit the Market** -- [Two lots on Congress Avenue](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/09/11/congress-ave-properties-redevelopment-sale-austin.html) have hit the market, including a former Marriott site with unrestricted height opportunity at the current Shiner Saloon location.

**State Theatre Renovation** -- The [historic 1935 State Theatre](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/09/11/state-theatre-downtown-austin-renovation.html) is receiving a spring renovation with structural updates and more amenities.
