## Data Dive

2024 median condo prices for downtown Austin by bedroom count:

- **One bedroom:** $566,500
- **Two bedrooms:** $815,000
- **Three bedrooms:** $1,810,000

## Stay in the Loop

**Hotel Trinity Demolition** -- [Demolition has begun](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/10/17/downtown-austin-hotel-trinity-renovation.html) at the Hotel Trinity site on the southwest corner of 5th and Trinity. The new hotel is expected to be completed in 2027.

**The Luminary Launches Sales** -- [The Luminary](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2024/10/03/luminary-condo-tower-austin.html) at 14th and Guadalupe begins sales in January. The 35-story tower will feature 293 condo units.

**Dia de Los Muertos** -- [Muertos Mercado](https://mexic-artemuseum.org/event/muertos-mercado/) at Republic Square featuring the Mexic-Arte Museum and Downtown Austin Alliance curated market.
