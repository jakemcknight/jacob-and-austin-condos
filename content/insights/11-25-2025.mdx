## Data Dive

One-bedroom units are experiencing the most significant year-over-year decline in downtown Austin's market. Entry-level buyers who would traditionally purchase one-bedroom units are increasingly choosing to rent in newly constructed high-rise towers instead, where landlords commonly offer multiple weeks of rental incentives.
