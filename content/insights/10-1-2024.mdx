## Data Dive

The downtown area currently houses 15,360 residents across 10,324 residential units, with 130,841 employees, according to the State of Downtown Austin 2024 report by the Downtown Austin Alliance.

## Stay in the Loop

**Tiger Lilly Replaces Estelle's** -- Tiger Lilly, a private events business, will occupy the two-story venue at 400 Colorado St. formerly home to Estelle's. Opening next month with public-facing elements planned.

**West 6th High-Rise Renderings** -- Kairoi Residential, which owns the WTF bar space, has released new renderings for a proposed high-rise development. It remains unclear whether the project will feature apartments or condos.

**301 Congress Porch** -- A vacant loading dock at 301 Congress has been transformed into a vibrant public space for community use.
