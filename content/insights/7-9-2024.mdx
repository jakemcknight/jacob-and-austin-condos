# Downtown Tree Canopy, Taco Fest, Barton Springs from 6G Tower

## Data Dive

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 210 (down 5% from 221)
- **New Listings:** 16 (down 20% from 20)
- **Pending:** 7 (up 43% from 4)
- **Closed:** 7 (up 57% from 3)

New supply decreased while demand picked up.

## Stay in the Loop

**ATX Tower Progress** -- The 54-story development has topped out with an estimated completion in 2025. The project includes 140,000 square feet of office space and 370 residential units above.

**Coworking Expansion** -- An industrial coworking firm has doubled its presence at the 5th and Colorado office building downtown, becoming the second-largest coworking operator in Austin after WeWork.

**Taco Fest on Sabine Street** -- Scheduled for Saturday, July 13th from 11am to midnight, the event features 15 bands, 15+ food vendors, and entertainment. Tickets are $10.
