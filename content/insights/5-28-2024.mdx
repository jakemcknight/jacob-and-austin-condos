# State of Downtown 2024, 5th & West Amenities

## Data Dive

Downtown Austin experienced significant residential growth in 2023. According to the Downtown Austin Alliance report, 1,303 new residential units were delivered in 2023 and 3,062 more are currently under construction, comprising both rental apartments and condos. Population increased by approximately 850 residents over the year. While young professionals (ages 25-34) remain the largest demographic group, the median age rose from 35 to 37 between 2022 and 2023.

### Downtown Market Biweekly Snapshot

- **Total Active Listings:** 214 (up 5% from 203)
- **New Listings:** 22 (down 12% from 25)
- **Pending:** 18 (down 28% from 25)
- **Closed:** 10 (up 43% from 7)

The market has cooled on both new supply and absorption.

**6th Street Redevelopment Plans** -- Grant Plaza announced plans for 258 residential units, restaurants, and retail across 1.8 acres between Fifth and Sixth streets (Red River to Sabine).
