## Data Dive

Downtown Austin listing metrics reveal important market dynamics:

- **Active listings:** 104 days on market at $809/SF
- **Failed listings:** 125 days on market at $837/SF
- **Closed sales:** 83 days on market at $670/SF (41 days when sold at/above list)

60 of 68 sales over the past three months closed below list at 91% of original asking. Well-priced units generate urgency; overpriced inventory moves slowly.

## Stay in the Loop

- **City Council approvals:** The 6.5-acre Ego's property south of the river is now approved as a planned unit development, and the Waterloo Records redevelopment is advancing.

- **Donn's Depot:** The historic train depot piano bar continues to maintain its relevance amid Austin's rapid changes.

- **Construction pause:** Approximately 260 unsold units remain across Modern, Vesper, and Linden towers. No new residential projects are expected to break ground soon.
