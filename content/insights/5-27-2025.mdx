## Stay in the Loop

- **I-35 Caps funding approved:** The city approved funding for support columns enabling caps between Cesar Chavez and Fourth St, Fourth and Seventh, and 11th to 12th -- potentially adding 15+ acres downtown. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/05/22/i35-cap-and-stitch-austin-funding.html)

- **West 6th Street tower progress:** Kairoi's proposed 66-story residential high-rise received density bonus program approval. No construction timeline has been announced yet. [Read more](https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/news/2025/05/14/kairoi-residential-austin-tower-sixth-street.html)

- **Swedish Hill expanding downtown:** Opening fall 2025 at the ground floor of 415 Colorado, a new apartment tower. [See the announcement](https://www.instagram.com/p/DHWWHzTR7B3/?hl=en)
